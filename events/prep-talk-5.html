Prep Talk - Discover the Difference

'Have you ever felt like Engineering is not your forte? Want to do something unrelated to the domain?'

IEEE PES SBC MBCET, as a part of IEEE Celebrations 2021, is conducting the next session of PrepTalks on ”Discover the Difference - A talk with the portraits of success”

Get inspired from our alumni, working in non-engineering domains, share their journey and experiences.

Open to all colleges.

Speakers:
Mr. Ramit Chennithala IRS,
Asst Commissioner, Income Tax, Bangalore [MBCET Batch (2010 - 2014)]

Dr. Krishnan Jeesha,
